Victim's advocate

Compensation and assistance after domestic violence

If you have been subjected to domestic violence, you are as a rule entitled to have a victim's advocate appointed who accompanies you from start to finish in the case. The state pays the legal fees.

Compensation

Compensation claim after domestic violence

If you have been subjected to domestic violence, you may, among other things, be entitled to permanent injury compensation (menerstatning), coverage of loss of income, loss of homemaking capacity, expenses and compensation for non-economic loss (oppreisning).

What is covered?

What is domestic violence?

Put simply, it concerns situations in which you have been mistreated by someone close to you. This can take the form of threats, coercion, deprivation of liberty, violence or other abuse.

Someone close to you can, for example, be a current or former spouse or cohabitant, your children or your parents.

Domestic violence can be difficult to talk about. You will meet us with confidentiality and respect, no matter how long it has been going on.

Experience
