Current vacancy

Advokatfirmaet Torgersen is hiring lawyers

Are you a committed lawyer who wants to make a difference and help people in challenging situations? Then this could be the job for you.

We specialise in personal injury compensation, violence compensation, criminal law and social security law, and have experienced strong growth in recent years. The firm is well established at Billingstad, in pleasant premises a few minutes' walk from bus and train. Well-being, individual development and knowledge sharing are high priorities for us.

We are now advertising one to two permanent positions (100%) as a lawyer at the office at Billingstad. An increased caseload means we would like to strengthen the personal injury and victim's advocate team, while also expanding our expertise in family and inheritance law.

We are looking for someone who has

  • Relevant experience. In criminal law or personal injury compensation – experience with inheritance and family law is an advantage.
  • Independence and teamwork. You work independently, but are also a good team player.
  • Good personal qualities. Conscientious, responsible and accurate, with a strong ability to get things done.
  • Good communication skills. Empathetic, solution-oriented and with a positive attitude that contributes to the working environment.

We offer

  • Competitive terms.
  • A flexible home-office arrangement.
  • The opportunity to take on subject-matter responsibility and to be part of developing the company.
  • An informal and pleasant working environment with a canteen and a gym.
How to apply

Send your application

Send your application, CV, academic transcript and references to managing director Anette Torgersen by email at@advokattorgersen.no. If you have questions, the managing director can be reached by phone 98 67 29 29. Start date as soon as possible – applications are assessed on a rolling basis.
