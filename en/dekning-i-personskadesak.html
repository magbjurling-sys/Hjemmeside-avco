Personal injury

Coverage of legal costs in your personal injury case

Are you entitled to have your legal costs covered after a personal injury? This is a question many people wonder about – and in most cases the answer is yes.

Overview

Who covers your legal costs?

Many people who are injured end up in a financially difficult situation precisely because of the personal injury. It is therefore important to know that the liable insurance company must, as a general rule, cover your legal costs on the same basis as other expenses you have as a result of the injury.

Different schemes

How the costs may be covered

  • Traffic-accident injury and occupational injury / occupational disease. As a general rule, you are entitled to have the liable insurance company cover necessary and reasonable legal costs in the matter.
  • Patient injury. When the NPE has accepted that there was a failure in the health care, the NPE covers necessary and reasonable legal costs for the calculation of compensation. In special cases, costs from the initial stage during the investigation of liability are also covered.
  • Leisure-time accident. You may also be entitled to coverage of legal costs, depending on the type of accident and which bodies have to be contacted.
  • Dispute in the matter. If a dispute arises with the insurance company or the Patient Injury Compensation Board, you may be entitled to coverage through the legal-expenses insurance (rettshjelpsdekning) in your home contents insurance.
Legal-expenses insurance

Coverage through the home contents insurance

The home contents insurance usually has legal-expenses cover with a sum insured of NOK 100,000. The excess is normally NOK 4,000 plus 20% of the amount above that, while the insurance company covers 80%. The exact amounts depend on the terms of your home contents insurance, and should be checked in the individual case.

As you can see, legal costs may be applied for through several schemes. If you tell us what your case concerns, we will give you an answer as to which coverage schemes are relevant for you. Lawyer Anette Torgersen has extensive experience with the various schemes for covering legal costs. Feel free to get in touch for a no-obligation conversation.
