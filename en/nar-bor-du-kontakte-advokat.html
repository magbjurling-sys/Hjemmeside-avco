Our advice

Contact a lawyer early in the process

We recommend that you contact a personal injury lawyer as soon as possible after an accident. You can then get an overview of your case and afterwards decide whether you wish to handle it on your own or let a lawyer assist you.

Several important choices are made along the way in a personal injury case, and these can have a major bearing on the outcome. What many people do not know is that in a number of personal injury cases the legal costs are covered already from the initial stage with the insurance company. There is therefore rarely any reason to wait before obtaining information.

Relevant case types

We assist with several types of personal injury

  • Traffic-accident injury. Compensation after a traffic accident through the vehicle's statutory insurance.
  • Occupational injury. Compensation after a workplace accident through the occupational injury insurance.
  • Occupational disease. Illness caused by harmful exposure in the working environment.
  • Patient injury. Compensation after a failure in the provision of health care, handled by the NPE.
  • Leisure-time accident. Compensation after an accident during your leisure time.

You are welcome to get in touch for a no-obligation conversation about your case.
