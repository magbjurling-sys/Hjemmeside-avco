We are specialists in personal injury

Advokatfirmaet Torgersen has specialist expertise in personal injury compensation, criminal injuries compensation and social security law. We work for people who have been injured or fallen ill, and who often face insurance companies and public bodies on their own. Our goal is for you to receive the compensation you are entitled to, and to feel that you are in safe hands throughout the entire process.

A personal injury case involves many choices and deadlines that can have a major bearing on the outcome. We go through your case thoroughly together with you, so that you understand your rights and which claims can be brought. What many people do not know is that the liable insurance company must, as a general rule, cover necessary and reasonable legal costs from the very initial stage.

Types of personal injury cases

Traffic-accident injury

Injured in traffic as a driver, cyclist or pedestrian? You may be entitled to compensation through the vehicle's statutory insurance.

Occupational injury

Compensation after a workplace accident through the employer's statutory occupational injury insurance – regardless of fault.

Occupational disease

Illness caused by harmful exposure in the working environment may give the same rights as an occupational injury.

Patient injury

Compensation after a failure in the provision of health care. The case is handled by the Norwegian System of Patient Injury Compensation (NPE).

Leisure-time accident

An accident during your leisure time may give rise to a right to compensation, depending on the type of accident and which insurance schemes you are covered by.

When should you contact a lawyer?

Contact a lawyer early in the process, so that no compensation claims are lost.

Which lawyer should you choose?

Choose a lawyer with experience in personal injury compensation and complex compensation calculations.

Coverage of legal costs

In most personal injury cases, necessary and reasonable legal costs are covered by a liable scheme.
