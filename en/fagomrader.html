What does it cost?

Legal costs are often covered

Many people put off getting in touch because they are worried about the costs. But in many cases, reasonable and necessary legal costs are covered by the opposing party's insurance company, legal expenses insurance, free legal aid or the public authorities.

Read more about prices and cover →

  • The insurance company's liability. Often covers legal costs once liability is acknowledged.
  • Legal expenses insurance. Home contents and other insurance policies cover many disputes.
  • Free legal aid. May be relevant in certain types of case and where income is low.
  • Victim's advocate. In cases of violence and abuse, the state often covers the lawyer.
