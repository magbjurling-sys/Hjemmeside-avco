About us

The experience and commitment that make the difference.

Advokatfirmaet Torgersen is built on substantial experience from leading law firms and an uncompromising specialisation in personal injury, compensation and social security law – with you as the client at the centre.

Speak with the lawyer Call us directly98 67 29 29
Anette Torgersen

Lawyer and owner

Lawyer Anette Torgersen founded the firm after many years as a lawyer at larger law firms. Today we are a team of lawyers, associate lawyers and legal assistants. The firm is based in Asker but takes on cases across the whole country. Meet the whole team →

She has specialised in personal injury compensation, criminal injuries compensation and social security law, and also holds an international master's degree with a specialisation in negotiation and mediation – an expertise that often leads to faster and better solutions for clients.

The firm works to safeguard the interests of people who have been injured or fallen ill – often the weaker party when facing insurance companies and the state. The aim is to contribute to greater awareness of rights, and to ensure that each individual client feels well looked after throughout the entire process.
