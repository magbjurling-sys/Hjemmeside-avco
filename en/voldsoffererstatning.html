Criminal injuries compensation

If you have been injured as a result of an act of violence, you may be entitled to compensation. Advokatfirmaet Torgersen has specialist expertise in criminal injuries compensation and will guide you safely through the entire process – from application to any appeal.

What is this?

Compensation for you who have been subjected to violence

Criminal injuries compensation is, in short, compensation for someone who has suffered a personal injury as a result of violence. If you have been subjected to a criminal act that has caused you injury, you may be entitled to have several types of losses and disadvantages covered.

We know that having been subjected to violence can be difficult and demanding. Our task is to take care of the legal side, so that you can focus on moving forward. You should not have to face the system alone.

How we help

Reliable assistance throughout your case

Lawyer Anette Torgersen has extensive experience with criminal injuries compensation cases and has specialised in this field. Among other things, we assist with:

  • Assessing whether you are entitled to criminal injuries compensation
  • Preparing and submitting the application to the Norwegian Criminal Injuries Compensation Authority (Kontoret for voldsoffererstatning, KFV)
  • Appealing any decision to reject your claim from the KFV
  • Clarifying whether your legal costs can be covered
