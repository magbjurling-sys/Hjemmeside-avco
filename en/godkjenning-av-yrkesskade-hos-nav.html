NAV / benefits

Approval of an occupational injury by NAV

An injury resulting from a workplace accident is called an occupational injury (yrkesskade). If the injury is approved by NAV, it brings several important advantages. We help you report and document the injury correctly.

Report the accident

How to report the workplace accident

If you sustain an injury as a result of a workplace accident, this is called an occupational injury. The accident should be reported both to the employer's insurance company and to NAV. The responsibility for reporting lies in principle with the employer, but this is unfortunately often neglected. It is therefore wise to make sure yourself that the injury is actually reported in both places.

It is recommended that you report the workplace accident to NAV as soon as possible.

Advantages

Why approval is important

When NAV approves the injury as an occupational injury, it brings several advantages:

  • You are exempt from the patient's contribution for doctor's visits related to the injury.
  • You may be entitled to claim permanent-injury compensation.
  • You may gain access to NAV benefits with special occupational-injury advantages.
