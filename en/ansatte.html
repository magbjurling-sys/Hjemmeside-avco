Lawyers

Our lawyers

Anette Torgersen

Lawyer / partner

Anette established Advokatfirmaet Torgersen in 2018 after many years as a lawyer at larger law firms, including Osloadvokatene and Codex Advokat, and as an associate lawyer at the Norwegian Financial Services Complaints Board (Finansklagenemnda). She has specialised in personal injury compensation, criminal injuries compensation and social security law, and holds an international master's degree (LLM) in negotiation and mediation from Bond University in Australia. Anette is a member of the Norwegian Bar Association's committee for victim's advocates and is particularly concerned with the rights of injured parties and victims of violence.

Email: at@advokattorgersen.no
Tel: 98 67 29 29
