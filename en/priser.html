Cover schemes

Legal costs are often covered

We have good knowledge of the various cover schemes. In many cases, neither you nor we pay for the legal assistance – the cost is covered by an insurance company, the public authorities or other schemes. Here are the most common:

  • The liable insurance company. In most personal injury cases – for example after a traffic accident or a workplace accident/occupational illness – the liable insurance company must cover necessary and reasonable legal costs.
  • The Norwegian System of Patient Injury Compensation (Norsk Pasientskadeerstatning, NPE). In patient injury cases where NPE has accepted that there was a failure in the healthcare provided, NPE covers necessary and reasonable legal costs for the work on assessing the compensation.
  • The public authorities (victim's advocate). In cases involving victims of violence where you are entitled to a victim's advocate, the public authorities cover the legal costs.
  • Free legal aid. For certain areas of law, free legal aid may be granted if you meet the conditions.
Fees

How the fee is calculated

Advokatfirmaet Torgersen follows the Norwegian Bar Association's guidelines for the pricing of legal services. Unless otherwise agreed, the fee is calculated according to time spent. The smallest unit of time recorded is 0.25 hours (15 minutes), and you receive a specified time sheet showing what the work has been used for.

The standard hourly rate – which covers all staff categories with us, from office staff and associate lawyers to lawyers – ranges from NOK 1 315 (NOK 1 643.75 incl. VAT) to NOK 3 000 (NOK 3 750 incl. VAT).

The prices are indicative and may have changed. The final price and cover are always clarified with you before we begin the work.
