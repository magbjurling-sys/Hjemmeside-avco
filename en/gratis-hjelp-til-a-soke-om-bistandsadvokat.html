How we help you

The application is written free of charge for you

Lawyer Anette Torgersen works as a victim's advocate on a daily basis and has long experience with this work. If you tell us what your case concerns, you will get an answer as to whether there are grounds to apply to the court for the appointment of a victim's advocate. Lawyer Torgersen will then write the application free of charge for you. If the court refuses the application, we do not charge for the work either.
