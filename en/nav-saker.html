NAV / benefits

NAV and social security law

Are you not receiving the benefits you are entitled to? Advokatfirmaet Torgersen has specialist expertise in social security law and assists with everything from applications and appeals to appeals before the National Insurance Court (Trygderetten). We take cases nationwide.

Our expertise

Help throughout the case

Illness or injury can entitle you to a range of benefits from the Norwegian Labour and Welfare Administration (NAV). The path to a correct decision is not always straightforward, and unfortunately incorrect decisions (vedtak) are made on a regular basis. It is therefore important to know your rights and to make use of your right to appeal (klage) within the deadline.

We assist both with the application itself, with appeals to the NAV Appeals Unit (NAV Klageinstans) and with appeals to the National Insurance Court (Trygderetten). In many cases the legal costs are covered in full or in part. Get in touch and we will take a closer look at your particular case.

Social security cases we help with

Sickness benefit

Sickness benefit is intended to cover lost income when you are injured or ill. We help you in the event of a refusal or a disagreement over the calculation.

Work assessment allowance

After one year of illness you may be entitled to AAP. We assist when NAV refuses the claim or calculates the benefit incorrectly.

Disability benefit

If your ability to earn an income is permanently reduced, you may be entitled to disability benefit. Lawyer Anette Torgersen has extensive experience with disability cases.

Approval of an occupational injury by NAV

An approved occupational injury brings several advantages. We help you report and document the injury correctly.

Permanent-injury compensation

Compensation for loss of quality of life following a permanent injury. We assist with the assessment and the choice of the right specialist.

Help appealing a NAV decision

A refusal is not necessarily the final word. Many decisions are reversed following a thorough appeal.

The National Insurance Court

When NAV has refused your claim twice, the case can be appealed to the National Insurance Court (Trygderetten). We handle appeal cases for you.

Coverage of legal costs

In many NAV cases the legal costs are covered through free legal advice or free legal representation. See what applies.
