About us

A law firm with practice areas within personal injury and benefits

Advokatfirmaet Torgersen works within personal injury, compensation and social security law. Lawyer Anette Torgersen established the firm after several years at larger law firms, and today the firm consists of lawyers, associate lawyers and legal assistants. We are based in Asker and take cases across the whole country.

We assist people who have been injured or fallen ill, often in cases against insurance companies and the public authorities. Our work is about making rights clear and taking care of the law, documentation and correspondence on behalf of the client.

Practice areas

The areas we work with

We have chosen to specialise within a few selected areas of law.

01

Personal injury

Compensation after traffic injury, occupational injury, patient injury and other accidents – both for financial loss and permanent injury (menerstatning).

02

Victim's advocate

Assistance for you if you have been subjected to violence or abuse, throughout the criminal case. The lawyer is usually covered by the public authorities.

03

Criminal injuries compensation

We help you apply for criminal injuries compensation and appeal decisions from the Norwegian Criminal Injuries Compensation Authority (KFV).

04

NAV / benefits

Denied sickness benefits, AAP or disability benefit? We quality-check the decision and take the appeal further – all the way to the Social Security Tribunal if necessary.

05

Family law

Parental responsibility, permanent residence and contact arrangements. We help you find good solutions for the children in a difficult time.

Costs

Legal costs are often covered by others

In many personal injury cases, the opposing party's insurance company is to cover reasonable and necessary legal costs once liability is established. In other cases, legal expenses insurance, free legal aid or the public authorities may be relevant. We go through the cover options in an initial conversation.

Frequently asked questions

What people wonder about most

What does it cost to use a lawyer?

In many personal injury cases, the opposing party's insurance company covers reasonable and necessary legal costs once liability is acknowledged. Otherwise, legal expenses insurance or free legal aid may be relevant. We clarify the cost picture with you in an initial conversation.

Do I have a case?

That is something we find out together. Get in touch for a no-obligation assessment, and we will take a closer look at your case.

Is it too late to make a claim?

Compensation claims are subject to limitation periods, but these often start later than people think. Do not assume it is too late – get in touch, and we will check the deadlines in your case.

Do I have to come to the office?

No. We take cases across the whole country and can handle most things by phone, video and email. The office is in Asker for those who would like to meet in person.

How long does a case take?

It varies with the complexity of the case. Many cases are resolved by settlement within months, while more extensive cases can take longer. We give you a realistic timeframe early on.
