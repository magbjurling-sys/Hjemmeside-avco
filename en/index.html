About us

An experienced team that is fully on your side

Advokatfirmaet Torgersen specialises in personal injury, compensation and social security law. Lawyer Anette Torgersen founded the firm after many years at larger law firms, and today we are a team of lawyers, associate lawyers and legal assistants who take cases across the whole country from our office in Asker.

We work for people who have been injured or fallen ill – usually the weaker party when facing insurance companies and the state. You should not have to fight alone. We carry the weight of the law, documentation and negotiations, so that you can spend your energy on getting well.

Practice areas

This is what we help you with

We take cases where your rights, your finances and your future are at stake.

01

Personal injury

Traffic injury, occupational injury, patient injury or another accident? We make sure you are compensated for both financial loss and permanent injury (menerstatning).

02

Victim's advocate

If you have been subjected to violence or abuse, we guide you safely through the entire criminal case – often with the state covering the cost.

03

Criminal injuries compensation

We help you apply for criminal injuries compensation (voldsoffererstatning) and appeal decisions from the Norwegian Criminal Injuries Compensation Authority (Kontoret for voldsoffererstatning, KFV).

04

NAV / benefits

Denied sickness benefits, work assessment allowance (AAP) or disability benefit? We quality-check the decision and take the appeal further – all the way to the Social Security Tribunal (Trygderetten) if necessary.

05

Family law

Parental responsibility, the child's permanent residence and contact arrangements. We help you find good solutions for the children in a difficult time.

What does it cost?

Legal costs are often covered – at no cost to you

In many personal injury cases, the opposing party's insurance company is obliged to cover reasonable and necessary legal costs once liability is established. In other cases, legal expenses insurance or free legal aid may apply. We go through the cover with you already in the first, no-obligation conversation – so that you know where you stand before you decide.

Frequently asked questions

What people wonder about most

What does it cost to use a lawyer?

In many personal injury cases, the opposing party's insurance company covers reasonable and necessary legal costs once liability is acknowledged. Otherwise, legal expenses insurance or free legal aid may be relevant. We always clarify the cost picture with you in the first, free conversation.

Do I have a case?

That is something we find out together. Get in touch for a no-obligation assessment, and we will quickly see whether you have a claim worth pursuing.

Is it too late to make a claim?

Compensation claims are subject to limitation periods, but these often start later than people think. Do not assume it is too late – get in touch, and we will check the deadlines in your case.

Do I have to come to the office?

No. We take cases across the whole country and can handle most things by phone, video and email. The office is in Asker for those who would like to meet in person.

How long does a case take?

It varies with the complexity of the case. Many cases are resolved by settlement within months, while more extensive cases can take longer. We give you a realistic timeframe early on.
