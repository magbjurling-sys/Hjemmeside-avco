Violence compensation

Weakened rights under a new act

Victim's advocate Anette Torgersen has, in the media – including on NRK – highlighted how the new Violence Compensation Act (voldserstatningsloven) has weakened the rights of people who are subjected to violence and abuse.

Compensation

Compensation even when the case is dropped

Many people believe they lose the right to compensation when the criminal case is dropped. That is not necessarily true – you may still be entitled to criminal injuries compensation (voldsoffererstatning).

Commitment

Member of the victim's advocate committee

In 2021, Anette Torgersen was appointed to the Norwegian Bar Association's Victim's Advocate Committee, and engages with the rights of victims and with the time the police spend on investigations.
