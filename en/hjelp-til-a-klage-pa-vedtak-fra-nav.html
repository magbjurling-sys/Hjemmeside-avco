Right of appeal

Make use of your right to appeal the decision

Unfortunately, a number of incorrect decisions are made at NAV. If you disagree with the decision you have received, it is important that you make use of your right to appeal, and that the appeal deadline is met.

Many refusals from NAV are reversed after a lawyer has submitted an appeal, so that the client nonetheless receives the benefit they are entitled to. A thorough and well-reasoned appeal often makes a major difference.

Legal costs

Are the legal costs covered?

Are the legal costs covered when appealing a NAV decision?

In many cases the legal costs of the work on the appeal are covered. We clarify what applies to your case when you get in touch. Read more on the page about coverage of legal costs in a NAV case.

Which cases do you assist with?

Lawyer Anette Torgersen specialises in social security law and has extensive experience appealing various types of refusal decisions from NAV, including those concerning sickness benefit, work assessment allowance and disability benefit.
