Victim's advocate

Compensation after sexual abuse of children

If you were subjected to sexual abuse as a child, you are as a rule entitled to have a victim's advocate appointed who accompanies you from start to finish in the case. The state pays the legal fees.

Compensation

Compensation claim after abuse

If you were subjected to sexual abuse as a child, you may, among other things, be entitled to permanent injury compensation (menerstatning), coverage of loss of income, loss of homemaking capacity, expenses and compensation for non-economic loss (oppreisning).

Speaking up

Report abuse

If someone has subjected you to sexual abuse or done something to your body that you did not want, it is important to speak up about it. It is common for it to take a long time before someone who has experienced abuse dares to tell about it. It is never too late to get in touch, and you will meet us with security and respect.

Experience

You are welcome to get in touch

Lawyer Anette Torgersen has long experience as a victim's advocate in abuse cases. She can, among other things, help you report the case, assist you during questioning, follow your case closely and bring your compensation claim.
