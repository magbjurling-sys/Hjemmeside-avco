Our advice

Choose a lawyer with experience in personal injury compensation

We recommend that you choose a lawyer who has experience with personal injury cases. Familiarity with how insurance companies and public bodies work in such cases is valuable, and experience with medical experts and specialised professional knowledge contributes to a better outcome.

The calculation of compensation claims can often be complicated. In that case it is an advantage that the lawyer has good knowledge of this field. We use specially adapted calculation software developed precisely for personal injury compensation and criminal injuries compensation.

Lawyer Anette Torgersen has extensive experience with personal injury and criminal injuries compensation cases, and uses tools that are adapted to these specialised claims.

This is what we look for

What characterises a good personal injury lawyer?

  • Experience with personal injury. The lawyer should work regularly with personal injury cases.
  • Familiarity with the opposing party. Insight into how insurance companies and public bodies work.
  • Medical understanding. Experience with medical experts and assessment of the consequences of an injury.
  • Accurate calculations. Good knowledge of complex compensation calculations and suitable tools.
